Bad

Only applying mouseover and mouseout events on the bar graphic results in a bug where the tooltip disappears when hovering over it.

This is a tooltip. Hover over me and I will disappear.

$('.bar').mouseover(function(){
    $(this).next('.bar__tooltip').show();
});

$('.bar').mouseout(function(){
    $(this).next('.bar__tooltip').hide();
});

Good

An example of using jQuery's $.fn.trigger() method to keep the tooltip shown when hovering over it.

This is also a tooltip. Hover over me and I should stay in place.

View More 
					
$('.bar__tooltip--trigger').mouseover(function(){
    $(this).prev('.bar').trigger('mouseover');
});

$('.bar__tooltip--trigger').mouseout(function(){
    $(this).prev('.bar').trigger('mouseout');
});
